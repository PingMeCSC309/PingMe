The app

Ping Me is the next big thing in social networking!
It allows you to know the location of your friends at any time on Google Maps.
Check out all the wonderful features Ping Me may or may not have below!

Pings (of course)
When you Ping, all of your friends get your current location.
cool Ping options
  • Ping requests to personally ask for one's location
  • Automatic Pinging with interval and schedule parameters
  • Proximity Pings to know that you're near a friend's Ping
  • Arrival Pings to alert that you've reached a destination
  • "Snap" Ping to attach a picture to your Ping
drawings
Connect the dots of your recent Pings to get a map of your journies!
hot Pings
See the popular (i.e. most Pinged) locations in your area or between your friends!
events and meetups
Track the location of evereyone RSVP'd before an Event. Be sure to arrive on time! Or, use Meetups to be notified of when your friend arrives at a destination.
races
The first person to Ping at a starting and finshing location wins! Create races between two points, challenge your friends and keep track of their best times.
this website
Which is mostly for information and very basic Ping visualization purposes. Download the Android mobile app for all these features!
